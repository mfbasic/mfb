NAME
  inZone - project an instant into a zone

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::inZone(at AS Instant, zone AS Zone) AS DateTime

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::inZone is the primary "to civil time" call: it projects the instant
  at through zone, resolving the effective UTC offset (DST applied for local
  zones) and storing it on the result. The returned DateTime carries the civil
  date and time, the zone, and the resolved offset, so it round-trips back to at
  via datetime::resolve without re-consulting the zone. datetime::toUtc and
  datetime::toLocal are shorthands for the utc and local zones.

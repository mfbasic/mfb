NAME
  date - validating Date constructor

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::date(year AS Integer, month AS Integer, day AS Integer) AS Date

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::date builds a proleptic-Gregorian Date, validating that month is in
  1 .. 12 and day is in range for that month and year (so February 29 is accepted
  only in leap years). Use it with datetime::time and datetime::civil to build a
  DateTime in a zone.

ERRORS
  An impossible calendar date fails with errorCode::ErrInvalidArgument
  (77050002), for example datetime::date(2026, 2, 29).

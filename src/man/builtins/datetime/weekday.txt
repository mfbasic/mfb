NAME
  weekday - the day of the week of a civil date

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::weekday(dt AS DateTime) AS Weekday

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::weekday returns the Weekday enum (Monday .. Sunday) for the civil date
  of dt.

SEE ALSO
  mfb man datetime dayOfYear, mfb man datetime format

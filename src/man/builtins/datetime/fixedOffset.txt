NAME
  fixedOffset - a fixed-offset zone

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::fixedOffset(offsetSeconds AS Integer) AS Zone
  datetime::fixedOffset(hours AS Integer, mins AS Integer) AS Zone

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::fixedOffset builds a Zone with a constant UTC offset. The one-argument
  form takes a raw second count; the two-argument form takes hours and a minute
  magnitude in 0 .. 59 that takes the sign of hours, so fixedOffset(-5, 30) is
  -05:30. The label is rendered as +/-HH:MM.

ERRORS
  An offset magnitude of 24 hours or more, or mins outside 0 .. 59, fails with
  errorCode::ErrInvalidArgument (77050002).

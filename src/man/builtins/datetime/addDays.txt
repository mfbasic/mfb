NAME
  addDays - calendar-day arithmetic on a civil DateTime

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::addDays(dt AS DateTime, days AS Integer) AS DateTime

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::addDays advances dt by a whole number of calendar days, keeping the
  wall-clock time of day and re-resolving the offset through dt's zone, so it stays
  DST-aware. days may be negative.

NAME
  civil - build a DateTime from civil parts in a zone

SYNOPSIS
  datetime::civil(date AS Date, time AS Time, zone AS Zone) AS DateTime

PACKAGE
  datetime

IMPORTS
  IMPORT datetime

DESCRIPTION
  datetime::civil builds a DateTime from a Date and Time interpreted in zone,
  resolving the UTC offset for that local time and returning the canonical
  projection (so the result round-trips through datetime::resolve).

  When the named local time does not exist (a spring-forward gap) or is ambiguous
  (a fall-back overlap), civil resolves deterministically: a gap shifts forward by
  the gap (the post-transition offset), and an overlap picks the earlier
  (pre-transition) offset. This is the common "lenient" convention.

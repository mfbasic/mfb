NAME
  floor - greatest integer less than or equal to a numeric value

SYNOPSIS
  math::floor(value AS Float) AS Integer
  math::floor(value AS Fixed) AS Integer

DESCRIPTION
  Returns the greatest integer less than or equal to value.

  The Float overload fails with ErrOverflow (77050010) when the result is outside
  Integer range.

EXAMPLES
  LET value AS Integer = math::floor(3.75)

NAME
  exp - e raised to a numeric power

SYNOPSIS
  math::exp(value AS Float) AS Float
  math::exp(value AS Fixed) AS Fixed

DESCRIPTION
  Raises e to value.

  The Fixed overload rounds to the nearest Fixed value and fails with
  ErrOverflow (10028) on overflow.

EXAMPLES
  LET value AS Float = math::exp(1.0)

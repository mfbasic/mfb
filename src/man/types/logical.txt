NAME
  logical - Boolean logical operators

SYNOPSIS
  NOT
  AND
  OR
  XOR

DESCRIPTION
  MFBASIC logical operators work on Boolean operands and return Boolean.

RULES
  NOT is unary and requires one Boolean operand.

  AND and OR short-circuit:
  AND skips the right operand when the left operand is FALSE.
  OR skips the right operand when the left operand is TRUE.

  XOR evaluates both operands and returns TRUE when exactly one operand is
  TRUE.

PRECEDENCE
  NOT has higher precedence than AND.
  AND has higher precedence than OR and XOR.
  XOR has the same precedence as OR.

EXAMPLES
  LET ready AS Boolean = NOT FALSE
  LET allowed AS Boolean = isAdmin OR hasToken
  LET changed AS Boolean = oldValue XOR newValue
